Welcome to the list

TBU This page is designed to help you learn how to use our list management system effectively. Explore the features and functionalities to get started with organizing your tasks efficiently. As user you will be able to create, copy, edit and share lists and items. It it also possible for other users to add items to shared lists.

Create Your List in 5 Steps (New User)

Lists Overview

  1. On the landing page, click "Create Your List Now!"
  2. On the next page, click the link to "Sign Up"
  3. On the next page, Click "Sign Up"
  4. Click "Add List"
  5. Enter the name and click "Create Shopping List"

Now your list is created, continue below with adding items.

Create Your Item For Your List

  1. Add item
  2. Item created
  3. Show list view with item, click "Show Lists" in the navbar
  4. View item from list view, click on the list title.
  5. Select item done status

Share List (Private/Public)

  • By setting the toggle switch to left or right, you can share your list:
    • Private
    • Public

Other functions

By using the "Edit" buttons you can also reach options to rename, delete, duplicate lists and items.
